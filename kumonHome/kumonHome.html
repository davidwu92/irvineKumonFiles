COVID-19 Update & Temporary Center Changes

Dear Kumon Family, We are writing to inform you that the Kumon centers in the Irvine and Tustin area have decided to temporarily suspend in-person sessions due to the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19).  This situation will last until things are deemed safe to reopen. Irvine Kumon (Barranca & Campus) will be suspending in-person class sessions starting Saturday 3/14/20.  However, our center will remain open and operational during regular business hours to provide HW pick-up and exchange for all families during this time.  All students who come to the center during this week will automatically receive 2 weeks of homework to take home.

Online Kumon Sessions are Now Live!

Book your child's online Kumon session here.  Session are hosted 1x weekly via Zoom due to decreased student-teacher ratios. The sessions are a fantastic opportunity to create a sense of routine, accountability, and regular teacher-interaction for your students studying at home. It is important that your child's education and progress is not interrupted during this extended period.  During this temporary class suspension, it is absolutely critical to home grade your homework daily. Your child's welfare is our utmost concern, and we will closely monitor the situation and will contact parents for continued updates.

Online Class Option

Online sessions are offered once a week.
  • Early Learners up to Kindergarten: 1:1 sessions (one teacher, one student). 15 min per subject.
  • Students from 1st Grade and up: 2:1 sessions (max. two students per teacher). 20 min per subject.

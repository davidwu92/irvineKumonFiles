COVID-19: Fall 2020 Update

We here at Irvine Kumon are ready to provide academic support for your child as their school year begins! Barranca and Campus Centers remain open (Monday-Friday, 9:00am-5:00pm) and fully-operational through online learning sessions and contact-free homework exchange.
We are also offering safe, Social-Distancing-enforced classes in person at our centers (available by appointment only). We're here to maintain healthy routines and keep your students on track in these times of uncertainty.

Online Kumon Sessions are Now Live!

Our small, uncrowded Zoom learning sessions are designed to foster a strong sense of routine, accountability, and regular teacher-interaction for your child. Existing Kumon parents should sign up ASAP!
  • Early Learners up to Kindergarten: 1:1 sessions (one teacher, one student).
  • Students from First Grade and up: 2:1 sessions (max. two students per teacher).
  • Sessions are now offered 1x weekly due to our reduced student-teacher ratio.

HOME-GRADING GUIDE

Summary: the 3-Step HW Exchange

  1. Request homework by submitting the online form (blue link above).
    2. Barranca and Campus centers have different HW Exchange Guidelines. Please read through your center's guidelines carefully!
  2. Make sure to home-grade your child's HW daily!
    3. Mistakes in HW must be addressed immediately; this is essential to your child's learning process.
  3. Drop off graded, completed homework at your center (as you pick up requested HW).
    4. Don't forget to write your child's name (Last, First) on anything you turn in.

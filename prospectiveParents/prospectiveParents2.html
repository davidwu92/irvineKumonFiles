Is Kumon Right for Your Child?

For 30 years, Irvine Kumon has proudly supported a wide range of learners with diverse ages, backgrounds, and abilities. Our math and reading programs cover content from preschool to 12th grade—from counting to calculus, phonemics to Shakespeare. But Kumon goes beyond tutoring: students actively develop critical thinking skills as they progress independently through our meticulously-crafted curriculum.

Our program may be perfect for your child if he or she is...

  • a young learner for whom you want to provide a jump-start in learning foundational skills.
  • falling behind and needs additional practice with a teacher who will provide consistent, timely feedback.
  • a student with average to excellent grades—ready to push their academics & depth of understanding to the next level.
  • searching for an individualized program to help identify & address "holes" in their mastery of prior math/reading concepts.
  • in need of a structured program that will help establish & reinforce healthy academic routines.

Prospective Parents' FAQ

Q: At what age can my child start at Irvine Kumon? How early is too early? A: Many of the students at our centers started Kumon at the age of 3 - 3.5. However, we have accepted students as young as 2.5 years old. As long as your child can 1. hold a pencil and 2. follow instructions, he or she is ready to join! The importance of prekindergarten education today cannot be overstated.

Q: Is every Kumon center the same? A: No. Our Irvine instructor Janie Choi has 30 years of experience in education. She established the first Kumon center ever in Irvine in 1990, and has since touched thousands of local students' lives. While the Kumon curriculum is consistent across North America, each Kumon center is an individually owned and managed franchise.  The Irvine Kumon centers on Barranca (in Woodbridge) and on Campus Drive (by UCI) are both owned and operated by Janie Choi and her experienced team. "Since Kumon is franchise, you would think they all should be the same, but no, by far this one in Irvine is the BEST, we drive from Anaheim for our kid to have this kind of atmosphere, great place." - Yelp Review 2014

Janie during her earlier years at Kumon.

Q: How long will it be before I see results in my child's progress? A: Kumon is designed to be a long-term program.  While your student's confidence in math and reading will grow every day with practice, the greatest benefits of Kumon typically become apparent after one year.  Every Irvine Kumon student will be able to advance beyond his or her grade level with patience and dedication.  Like with sports and music, there is no such thing as a short-cut to success!

Q: How do I enroll my student at Irvine Kumon? A: We're currently asking prospective parents to email or text us to schedule a free one-to-one Zoom session with us for your Parent Orientation and Student Testing. Prior to the COVID-19 outbreak, we would invite parents for an in-person Parent Orientation and Student Testing at one of our centers.
